Where Africa

✦ UNESCO World Heritage Site 2006

Harar Jugol — the walled city — is a living museum of Islamic architecture, Harari culture, and ancient trade routes that once connected the African interior to the Arabian Peninsula. Its labyrinthine lanes hide over 350 historic buildings, centuries-old mosques, and a hyena-feeding tradition unlike anywhere on earth.

This is the official tourism portal of the Harari Regional Tourism Bureau — your trusted, authoritative guide to visiting one of Africa's most extraordinary cities.

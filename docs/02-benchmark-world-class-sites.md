## Feature patterns across leaders

### 1. Discovery & exploration

- Interactive maps with filterable POIs
- Attraction detail pages with rich media, hours, tips
- Category and interest-based browsing (heritage, food, nature, festivals)
- Global search across all content types
- "Explore by interest" landing pages

### 2. Planning & practical info

- Dedicated "Plan your trip" hubs (visas, transport, best time, costs)
- Day itineraries and suggested routes
- Events calendar (not just news list)
- Downloadable / offline guides
- Emergency and safety information

### 3. Booking & action

- Guide and tour booking (direct or partner-integrated)
- Attraction passes or ticket links
- Hotel and restaurant directories
- Real-time or request-based availability

### 4. Engagement & trust

- Multi-language support
- User accounts, saved favorites, shared itineraries
- User-generated content / social proof
- AI trip assistants (emerging)
- Mobile apps with push notifications

### 5. Admin & ecosystem (behind the scenes)

- Tourism-specific CMS with draft/publish workflows
- Role-based editors (including external partners)
- Analytics dashboards for staff
- Partner/business portals
- Audit trails and editorial approval flows

## Content model: Hero – Hub – Hygiene

Used by Montenegro and other top DMO sites:

| Layer | Purpose | Visit Harar examples |
|-------|---------|---------------------|
| **Hero** | Stunning visuals, emotional hook | Homepage hero, gallery |
| **Hub** | Stories, personas, curated guides | Culture page, featured attractions, news |
| **Hygiene** | Practical, factual, always needed | Plan Your Trip, contact, booking, map |

**Action:** Audit current pages against this model in [UI/UX Investigation](./08-ui-ux-investigation.md).

## Persona-based navigation (Visit Dubai pattern)

Leading sites branch content for different traveler types:

| Persona | Harar-relevant content |
|---------|------------------------|
| First-time international visitor | UNESCO intro, visa, Dire Dawa transfer, guided tours |
| Domestic Ethiopian tourist | Amharic content, local prices, weekend trips |
| Spiritual / heritage traveler | Mosques, shrines, festivals, respectful visiting guides |
| Adventure / unique experiences | Hyena feeding, coffee ceremony, markets |
| Photographer / culture enthusiast | Gallery, Rimbaud, weaving, alleyways |

**Future:** Persona landing pages or homepage entry points — discuss in UI/UX phase.

## What we should NOT copy blindly

- Dubai-scale pass/ticket ecosystems (different market maturity)
- 10-language launch (start with 2–3 strategic languages)
- Heavy partner self-service portals (start commission-managed)
- AR/VR gimmicks before core planning works

**Win on:** Authentic Harar depth, walkable-city navigation, spiritual heritage, and truly being the one stop for Harar — not generic DMO template.

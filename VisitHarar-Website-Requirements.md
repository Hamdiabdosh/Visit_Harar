# VisitHarar Website Requirements

## Project Vision

**Website Name:** VisitHarar

**Mission**

VisitHarar is the official tourism portal of Harari Region that showcases Harar's history, culture, heritage, cuisine, festivals, nature, and hospitality while making it easy for domestic and international travelers to plan their visit.

---

# Primary Goals

* Increase tourism arrivals
* Promote local businesses
* Preserve Harari heritage
* Help tourists plan trips
* Encourage longer stays
* Promote sustainable tourism

---

# Visual Design

The visual identity should reflect Harari's unique character:

* Warm earth tones inspired by Harari architecture.
* Rich colors influenced by traditional Harari baskets and textiles.
* Large, immersive photography and cinematic video.
* Elegant typography with clear readability.
* Decorative geometric motifs inspired by Harari craftsmanship, used subtly throughout the interface.
* Smooth animations that enhance, rather than distract from, the browsing experience.

---

# Future Enhancements

* AI travel assistant for personalized recommendations.
* Online booking for guided tours and cultural experiences.
* Digital tourist pass with bundled attraction tickets.
* Augmented Reality (AR) experiences at heritage sites.
* 360° virtual tours of the old city and museums.
* Community portal where local businesses can manage their own listings.
* Visitor reviews and ratings.
* Multilingual audio guides.
* Mobile app synchronized with saved trips and itineraries.

This structure would position **VisitHarar** as a modern, official destination portal comparable to leading tourism websites such as those of Singapore, Dubai, or New Zealand, while highlighting the distinctive heritage of Harar and serving both visitors and local tourism stakeholders.
